About Us

Bungel Nigeria Limited is a prominent retailer and distributor of petroleum products, specializing in petrol and gas. Our extensive network of fuel stations serves numerous customers, providing them with top-quality fuel.

In addition to retail services, we supply fuels like petrol and gas to other fuel stations, ensuring a reliable and consistent fuel supply across the region. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives us to maintain the highest standards in the industry.

We also excel in the real estate sector, offering a range of services including property management, sales, and development. Our experienced team is dedicated to delivering innovative and efficient real estate solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

At Bungel Nigeria Limited, our core values of integrity, innovation, and customer-centricity drive us to continuously improve and adapt to the changing market demands, ensuring that we remain at the forefront of both the fuel and real estate industries.
